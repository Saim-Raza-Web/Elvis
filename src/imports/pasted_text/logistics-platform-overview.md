Complete Project Overview – Logistics, Fulfillment & Warehouse Management Platform



I am looking to build a complete logistics and fulfillment management platform that allows me to manage all warehouse operations, inventory, ecommerce orders, customer stock, shipments, billing, accounting, CRM, and business activities from a single system.



The platform should be developed as a modern SaaS solution that can be used by multiple companies and later commercialized or licensed to other businesses. The system must be independent and self-contained, meaning all essential business functions should be available within the platform without relying on external ERP software.



Multi-Company Management



The platform should support multiple companies operating within the same system while keeping their data completely separated. Each company should have its own users, warehouses, inventory, customers, orders, reports, billing, and settings.



Warehouse Management



The warehouse management section should allow us to organize and control all warehouse activities efficiently. We need the ability to create products, manage warehouse locations, track stock levels, perform inventory audits, process stock adjustments, manage internal transfers, and monitor inventory movements.



Warehouse locations should be configurable and include receiving zones, pallet storage areas, picking zones, packing stations, shipping areas, return areas, and blocked stock locations.



The system should always know the exact location of every product stored in the warehouse.



Barcode & QR Code Operations



Products, warehouse locations, pallets, and operational processes should support barcode and QR code scanning. Warehouse workers should be able to perform their tasks by scanning items instead of manually entering information, reducing errors and improving speed.



Receiving Operations



When goods arrive at the warehouse, staff should be able to receive products, validate quantities, compare deliveries against expected quantities, record discrepancies, record damaged products, and assign storage locations.



The system should maintain a complete record of every receiving operation.



Intelligent Storage Management



The software should help warehouse staff determine the most suitable storage location for products. Location suggestions should consider product categories, ownership, warehouse capacity, storage rules, operational requirements, and available space.



Inventory Management



Inventory should be visible in real time and managed accurately across all warehouses and locations.



The platform should support:



Available stock

Reserved stock

Blocked stock

Ecommerce stock

Third-party stock

Customer-owned stock

Internal company stock

Pallet stock



The system should always show inventory ownership, quantity, location, and status.



Multi-Owner Inventory



The platform should support fulfillment operations where multiple customers store inventory inside the warehouse. Each customer's inventory must remain separated and traceable while being managed within the same warehouse environment.



The system should clearly identify ownership of every product and every inventory movement.



Inventory Transfers & Replenishment



Warehouse staff should be able to move inventory between locations and warehouses. The system should record all transfers and support replenishment workflows where stock is moved from storage areas into picking locations when inventory becomes low.



Order Management



The platform should receive and manage orders from multiple sales channels. Orders should be automatically imported, validated, processed, and tracked throughout the fulfillment process.



The system should support order allocation, inventory reservation, status updates, and fulfillment workflows.



Ecommerce & Marketplace Integration



The platform should integrate with ecommerce stores and marketplaces, allowing products, inventory, orders, and shipment information to remain synchronized.



The system should support multi-channel selling and centralized management of all ecommerce operations from a



single dashboard.



Picking Operations



Warehouse workers should receive picking instructions that indicate which products need to be collected, the required quantities, and their exact warehouse locations.



Barcode validation should be used during picking to reduce mistakes and improve order accuracy.



Packing Operations



Before orders are shipped, staff should be able to verify products, validate quantities, and complete packing operations using scanning and verification processes.



Shipping Management



The system should manage the shipping process from order preparation through delivery.



The platform should support:



Shipment creation

Shipping label generation

Tracking number generation

Shipment monitoring

Delivery tracking

Shipping status updates

Carrier Integrations



The software should integrate with multiple shipping providers and logistics companies, allowing shipments to be managed directly from the platform.



Carrier integrations should support shipment creation, label generation, tracking synchronization, and delivery updates.



Automatic Carrier Selection



The platform should be capable of automatically selecting the most suitable shipping carrier based on destination, cost, delivery commitments, package size, weight, and operational rules.



Returns Management



The system should manage product returns and reverse logistics operations.



Returned products should be inspected, classified, approved or rejected, and then returned to inventory or moved into blocked stock depending on their condition.



CRM Module



The platform should include customer relationship management functionality for managing leads, prospects, customers, companies, activities, communications, notes, and follow-up tasks.



This will allow all customer information and interactions to be managed from a single system.



Billing & Invoicing



The software should include billing capabilities that allow invoice creation, invoice management, payment tracking, customer billing records, tax handling, and financial documentation management.



Accounting Module



The platform should include accounting functionality to manage income, expenses, financial records, accounting reports, operational costs, and financial visibility across the business.



Reporting & Analytics



Management dashboards should provide visibility into all areas of the business.



Reports should include:



Inventory reports

Warehouse performance reports

Order reports

Ecommerce reports

Sales reports

Logistics reports

Shipment reports

Customer reports

Financial reports

Operational reports



Dashboards should present key business metrics in a clear and easy-to-understand format.



User Management & Permissions



The platform should allow the creation of multiple user roles with configurable permissions.



Different users should only have access to the information and functionality required for their responsibilities.



Activity Logs & Traceability



Every action performed inside the system should be recorded.



The platform should maintain a complete history of:



Product movements

Inventory changes

Warehouse operations

User actions

Order processing activities

Shipping activities

Returns processing



This ensures complete operational traceability and accountability.



SaaS Administration



Because the platform will eventually be offered to other businesses, administrative functionality should be available to manage companies, users, subscriptions, platform settings, and overall system operations.



Multi-Language Support



The platform should support multiple languages, including:



Spanish

English

French

Italian



Users should be able to work in their preferred language without affecting other users.



Mobile & Warehouse Devices



The system should work efficiently on:



Desktop computers

Tablets

Android PDA devices

Barcode scanners

Warehouse handheld devices



Warehouse operations should be optimized for scanning and mobile usage.



Security



The platform should include secure authentication, user access controls, audit logging, data protection, backups, and permission management to ensure operational security



and data integrity.



Future Expansion & Compliance



The architecture should allow future modules, integrations, and business requirements to be added easily.



The system should also be prepared for future electronic invoicing and Spanish tax compliance requirements, including support for future VeriFactu integration.



Final Goal



The objective is to create a complete business platform that combines warehouse management, fulfillment operations, inventory control, ecommerce management, logistics management, shipping operations, customer management, billing, accounting, reporting, and multi-company SaaS functionality into a single modern and scalable solution that can support both internal operations and future commercial use.
